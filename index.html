Ornithology is the scientific study of birds, focusing on their behavior, ecology, physiology, classification, and conservation. It examines aspects such as bird migration, breeding patterns, vocalizations, and their roles in ecosystems. This field plays a crucial role in biodiversity conservation, as birds are key indicators of environmental health.
